the tomato method1, also known in spanish as the pomodoro method2 is a method of doing work where you give yourself exactly 25 minutes to work. it’s something you can beat the timer with. now, the magic of the tomato is that… you have 25 minutes to finish whatever the crap you are doing. it pushes you to work faster. it’s also a good measure of time. sometimes things take 1 tomato to finish, other times it takes 2. preferably, don’t take more than 2 tomatoes to finish a task.

also it keeps you in check. don’t get carried away doing study.
