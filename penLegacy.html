pentel techniclick 0.5

this is not a pen, it’s a mechanical pencil. but it was my old reliable for a very long time now. and it’s a solid pencil (9/10). it works well and it’s got a funny push mechanism. a good amount of pencil lead will leave you writing for months1. the only criticism i have is that it is kind of easy to press the button, but that’s only a little thing. also be careful with the clipping thing, because it’s really easy to break. some of my friends2 also used to use this and all of their clipping things broke as well.

bic cristal

literally everyone knows this pen. not much to say. cheap, writes everywhere, but it’s a ballpoint pen, and it hurts to write with.

20/203-5 because hand hurt ;-(.

m&g en-gel k-35

i got this as a gift. much different to bic cristal, this is a gel pen. and so it writes well, and is fluid. sometimes it doesn’t write, but that’s okay. apart from that, not too much to add tbh. it also has a very satisfying click sound :-).

solid 37/35 (-0.2 for not writing sometimes).
