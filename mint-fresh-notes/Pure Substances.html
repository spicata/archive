Pure means that a substance has a constant composition and a fixed properties. It is divided into elements and compounds. Elements are just the elements on the periodic table1. Compounds are made up elements, and they have a fixed atomic ratio. For example, table salt’s chemical compound2 is NaCl, meaning that it always has one sodium atom for each one chlorine atom.

Compounds can be organic or not that3. Organic compounds are compounds containing carbon that is produced by organisms. Inorganic compounds are not produced by organisms. It often has a metal and a non-metal. For example, NaCl is an inorganic compounds. Metal is always first.4 It can also have polyatomic ions.
