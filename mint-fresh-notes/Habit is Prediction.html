We get our dopamine during craving, not reward1. It’s because we can predict that good things2 ahead of us, and we get excited in anticipation. When the reward comes, our brains say: “Oh, I was already expecting this. No dopamine hit.”

  2. Good things for our monkey brain. Things like eating, reproducing, and other primitive stuff. We know beyond that, except our brains are still wired this way. ↩
