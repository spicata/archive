Last modified: 2023-07-10

Table of Contents

Every particle has a different size. This is extremely useful when we want to separate them. If the particles are just solid, we can use a sieve to separate it based on the size of its particles. Larger particles get caught up, smaller particles make it through. If the particles are in an aqueous suspension, we can just spin it very fast1 to collect larger particles at the bottom2.
