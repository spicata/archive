## The Name

Do:

- Full name: spicata mentha
- First name only: spicata
- Initials: M.spicata, Mspicata

Don't:

- Spicata Mentha
- Spicata
- S.mentha

## The Website Name

Do:

- URL: spicata.99000000.xyz
- Short-name: spicata 99
- Slightly-longer-name: spicata 99 mil
- Slightly-longerer-name: spicata dot 99 million

Don't:

- Be dumb.
